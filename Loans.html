LOAN APPROVAL AND REPAYMENT STRUCTURE

Qualifying for a Loan

To be eligible for loan approval, you must demonstrate a commitment to financial discipline. Individuals interested in obtaining a loan through Abroad Connect Bank need to adhere to the following criteria:

Loan Calculation and Disbursement

1. Savings Duration: You should have maintained an active savings account for a minimum of 6 months without any withdrawals on your saving account. This demonstrates your dedication to building financial stability.

2. Loan Application: After saving consistently for at least 6 months or above, you are eligible to apply for a loan. The amount you have saved in your account during this period will be the basis for your loan eligibility.

Loan Calculation and Disbursement

Upon meeting the qualifying criteria, your savings will be leveraged to support your loan application:

1. The amount of money saved in your account during the 6-month period will be multiplied by an impressive 1000%.

2. The multiplied amount will be deposited into your loan account as the approved loan amount.

3. You will gain access to a "Withdraw" button in your loan account, allowing you to access the funds.

Loan Repayment and Profits

Once you receive your loan, you will begin the repayment process:

1. Monthly Profits: The loan amount you took out will generate profits for Abroad Connect Bank at a rate of 2.5% per month.

2. Partial Loan Pay Instalments: Every month, you will be required to pay back a portion of the loan, consisting of the monthly profits (2.5% of the loan amount) and an additional 4% of the loan amount (Principal).

Penalty for Delayed Instalments

Timely repayment is crucial to maintain the integrity of the loan structure...

1. Penalties: Failure to pay your instalment on time will result in penalties.

2. Maximum Repayment Period: If penalties accumulate, you will have a maximum period of only 2 years (25 instalments) to fully clear the loan.

Clearing the Loan and Unlocking Benefits

Upon successfully repaying the loan within the specified timeframe, you will be eligible for various benefits...

1. Access to Savings: Once your loan balance reaches zero, you will regain access to your savings funds, allowing you to withdraw if desired.

2. Property Ownership and Investment Opportunities: Having cleared your loan, you can explore property ownership plans and investments offered by Abroad Connect Bank.

Generating Profits during Loan Clearance

As you work towards clearing your loan, there are opportunities to generate additional profits for yourself...

1. Profits for Loan Clearance: For every part of the total loan amount you pay back within the 2-year period, you will earn profits of 0.25%.

2. Withdrawal after Loan Clearance: These profits can only be withdrawn after your loan balance reaches zero.
